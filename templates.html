How to Use These Email Signatures

  1. Click on the signature template you prefer above to open it in a new window
  2. Select all content on the signature page (press Ctrl+A or Cmd+A)
  3. Copy the selected content (press Ctrl+C or Cmd+C)
  4. Go to your email client's signature settings
  5. Paste the copied content into your email signature field (press Ctrl+V or Cmd+V)
  6. Save your signature settings
