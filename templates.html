How to Use These Email Signatures

  1. Select your name from the dropdown menu above.
  2. Click on the signature template you prefer to open it in a new window.
  3. Select all content on the signature page (press Ctrl+A or Cmd+A).
  4. Copy the selected content (press Ctrl+C or Cmd+C).
  5. Go to your email client's signature settings.
  6. Paste the copied content into your email signature field (press Ctrl+V or Cmd+V).
  7. Save your signature settings.
